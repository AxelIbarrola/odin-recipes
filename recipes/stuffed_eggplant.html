Stuffed Eggplant

Description

This stuffed eggplant recipe is healthy, filling, and will please even the pickiest eaters.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant:You will need one eggplant (halved lengthwise) to make two servings.

  • Olive oil:Brush the shells with olive oil before seasoning with salt and pepper.

  • Seasoning:This stuffed eggplant is seasoned with salt, pepper, fresh garlic, and fresh basil.
